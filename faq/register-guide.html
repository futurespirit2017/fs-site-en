Delivery Flow

  1. Pre-application Application form
  2. Contact from our representative
  3. Proposal of optimal plan

    Proposal of server plan that fits your company's scale

    Whether it is an e-commerce site or corporate website, we will propose a server with specifications that best fit your capacity needs, considering factors such as contents, traffic, load of mail server, attachment file size etc.

  4. Main application
  5. Completion of setup
  6. After service

    We provide support on various server configurations as well as support on the usage of the control panel.

    We provide support on a wide range of requests, such as server configurations (domain・SSL certificate settings), support on the usage of control panel, DNS switchover・domain transfer, troubleshooting in case of trouble (year-round, 24-hour port monitoring)
